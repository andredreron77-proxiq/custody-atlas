Enhance the Custody Atlas Case Workspace by adding a smart "Next Best Step" panel at the top of the dashboard.

Goal:
Help users understand exactly what to do next based on where they are in the product journey.

Requirements:

1. Create a reusable component called NextBestStepPanel

2. Place it near the top of the Case Workspace, directly below the JurisdictionContextHeader if present

3. The panel should show a primary recommended next action based on available user context

4. Support these recommendation scenarios:

A. No jurisdiction yet
Show:
Title: "Start by finding your jurisdiction"
Description: "Enter your location to see the custody laws that may apply where you live."
Primary button: "Find my location"

B. Jurisdiction exists but no AI questions asked yet
Show:
Title: "Ask your first custody question"
Description: "Get a plain-English explanation of custody law based on your state and county."
Primary button: "Ask a question"

C. Jurisdiction exists but no document uploaded yet
Show:
Title: "Upload your custody order"
Description: "Analyze your document to identify key terms, important dates, and possible next questions."
Primary button: "Analyze a document"

D. User has uploaded a document but has not asked a follow-up document question
Show:
Title: "Ask about your document"
Description: "Follow up on your document summary to better understand what it means."
Primary button: "Ask about this document"

E. User has used AI and uploaded at least one document
Show:
Title: "Explore the Custody Law Map"
Description: "Compare custody laws across states and better understand how rules differ."
Primary button: "Open Custody Map"

5. Add an optional small secondary action link such as:
- "Skip for now"
- "View workspace"

6. Styling:
- visually prominent card
- professional legal-tech appearance
- soft accent background using the existing blue palette
- clear title, description, and CTA
- mobile responsive

7. Data handling:
- Use existing real state where available
- If full persistence is not yet implemented, use current page/session state or a placeholder decision engine
- Keep the logic modular so it can later be replaced with real analytics/user progress tracking

8. Add a small label above the card:
"Recommended Next Step"

9. Keep all existing dashboard cards and functionality intact

10. After implementation:
Summarize:
- component created
- logic used for recommendations
- where it was added
- which scenarios are currently powered by real data vs placeholders
